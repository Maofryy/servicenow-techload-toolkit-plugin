# Meeting Minutes Template

Standard output format for the Meeting Scribe skill.
Adapt all fields in [brackets] to the specific meeting.
This template is designed to be copy-pasted into an email without reformatting.

---

MEETING MINUTES
[Meeting Title]  |  [Date]  |  [Platform / Room]

Attendees:   [Name, Role]; [Name, Role]
Facilitator: [Name]
Purpose:     [One-sentence objective]

----------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
[2–3 sentences. What was resolved and why it matters.
Written for a reader who was not in the room.]


DECISIONS
1. [Declarative statement of what was agreed, unambiguously]
2. [...]


ACTION ITEMS

1. [Verb-led, specific, unambiguous action]
   Owner: [Name]   |   Due: [Date]

2. [...]
   Owner: [Name]   |   Due: TBD *

* Owner to confirm at next touchpoint.


OPEN ITEMS
Items requiring a decision before the next milestone:
1. [Item] — [Responsible party]


NOTES
[Supporting context, referenced documents, deferred discussion. "None" if none.]
